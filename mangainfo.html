Jujutsu Kaisen Manga

Overview

Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami. It has been serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shonen Jump since March 2018. The story follows high school student Yuji Itadori as he joins a secret organization of Jujutsu Sorcerers to eliminate a powerful Curse named Ryomen Sukuna, of whom Yuji becomes the host.

Volume 1

The first volume introduces Yuji Itadori, a high school student with extraordinary physical abilities. After encountering a cursed object, Yuji's life changes forever as he becomes involved in the world of Jujutsu Sorcery. The volume sets the stage for the epic battles and dark themes that define the series.

Volume 2

In the second volume, Yuji begins his training at the Tokyo Metropolitan Magic Technical College under the guidance of Satoru Gojo. He meets fellow students Megumi Fushiguro and Nobara Kugisaki, and together they face their first mission as Jujutsu Sorcerers. The volume delves deeper into the mechanics of curses and sorcery.

Volume 3

The third volume continues the story with more intense battles and character development. Yuji and his friends face new challenges and powerful curses, testing their skills and resolve. The volume also explores the backstory of some key characters, adding depth to the narrative.

Volume 4

In the fourth volume, the story intensifies as Yuji and his friends face even more dangerous curses. The volume introduces new characters and reveals more about the world of Jujutsu Sorcery.

Volume 5

The fifth volume continues to build on the story, with Yuji and his friends encountering new challenges and powerful enemies. The volume delves deeper into the characters' backgrounds and motivations.
